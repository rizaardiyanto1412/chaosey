How To Play

  • One room supports up to four players, each controlling one of `W`, `A`, `S`, or `D`.
  • Create a room, choose public or private, then share the four-letter code if needed.
  • The host can start as soon as at least two players are in, or wait for a full four-player team.
  • Guide the squirrel around hazards and reach the goal together.
